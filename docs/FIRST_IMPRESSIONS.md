# First Impressions & Marketing

**.io** = product / try-it moment. **.com** = marketing site. First impressions on .io still need to convert.

---

## What Makes This a Marketing Machine

### 1. One clear promise above the fold
- Headline + one line of benefit. No clutter.
- Current: "What would you like to build?" + "Describe your business. Your site appears in seconds." ✓

### 2. One primary CTA
- Single obvious action: describe → GENERATE. No competing buttons.

### 3. Trust in under 5 seconds
- "X sites built today" + "No credit card · HTML export · 1-click deploy" = instant credibility.
- Optional: one short testimonial or "As seen in" if you have it.

### 4. Speed and polish
- Gold fog + glass dock + fast load = premium feel.
- First paint fast; generator responds in under ~30s so the promise is kept.

### 5. Reduce friction to "try"
- No signup to generate. Optional account only when they want to save/deploy.

### 6. .io → .com handoff
- Nav links (Templates, Gallery, Pricing, About) can go to .com for deeper marketing.
- Footer same. One "Learn more" or "See how it works" that goes to .com is enough.
